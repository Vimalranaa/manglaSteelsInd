About us

About Us

Mangla Steels is a committed and trusted brands of scaffolding industry manufacturer, exporter, Service Provider, Contractor and repair of the quality- assured range of products in compliance with the international standards. Mangla Steels also grants the products for rental purposes. The rental facility is for those clients whose projects are time based and needs it to be done at the earliest. The focus is not to manufacture the products but also deliver the best service to the clients that builds long-term relationships. With a team dedicated towards its objectives, Mangla Steels believes in the adoption of latest technology machines and equipment. The extensive range of products is widely appreciated by the clients for its sturdy standards, dimensional precision, weathering resistance, excellent wear strength and perfect design and finish. Our R&D unit helps us to improve our methods of production and fix the lapses in the product and services. We also ensure timely delivery of the ordered products via our worldwide distribution channels. Mangla Steels is SBU (Strategic Business Unit) which is consistent in delivering the classified products and supply since 2008.
